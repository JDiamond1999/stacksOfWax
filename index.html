Who are we?

Stacks of Wax is a website that connects music lovers with vinyl records. It provides a platform for users to discover and explore new and vintage albums. The website allows users to view album information, including tracklists, artwork, and artist details. Stacks of Wax also features a comprehensive database of vinyl records, making it easy for users to find what they're looking for. In addition to browsing records, users can leave reviews and ratings on albums they have listened to. This helps other users make informed decisions about what records to add to their collection.

The website is user-friendly and designed for easy navigation, making it accessible to both vinyl enthusiasts and newcomers to the format. Stacks of Wax also offers recommendations based on users searches. Users can create custom lists of their favorite records, which can be shared with others on the website. Stacks of Wax is constantly updated with new records and information, ensuring that users have access to the latest releases

The website also offers a blog section, where users can read articles and news about vinyl records and the music industry. Stacks of Wax is a great resource for both casual listeners and serious collectors, offering a wide range of albums and genres. The website's community-driven approach encourages users to share their knowledge and opinions about vinyl records. Stacks of Wax is an essential tool for anyone looking to expand their vinyl collection and discover new music. The website is also a great place to chat about records in our reviews section records. Stacks of Wax is committed to promoting the vinyl format and preserving the rich history of recorded music. The website is constantly improving and expanding, making it an essential resource for all vinyl enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting out, Stacks of Wax is the perfect place to explore and discover the world of vinyl records. So, if you're passionate about music and vinyl, be sure to check out Stacks of Wax today! Join the community and discover the magic of vinyl records!
